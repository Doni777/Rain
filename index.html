FAQ

Common Questions

Rain Protocol is a decentralized prediction market infrastructure on Arbitrum. It operates as the "Uniswap of prediction markets" — permissionless, open, and powered by the deflationary $RAIN governance token. Anyone can create markets, trade outcomes, and participate in governance.
$RAIN is the utility and governance token on Arbitrum. It's required to access the app, participate in governance, and earn Trading Power — which amplifies your position capacity. 2.5% of all protocol trading volume is used to buy and permanently burn $RAIN, creating deflationary pressure as usage grows.
Connect your wallet using the Claim section above. Enter your wallet address to check eligibility based on the November 2025 snapshot. Eligible wallets include early users, on-chain traders, and community contributors. If eligible, sign the claim transaction and $RAIN will be sent directly to your wallet.
Rain uses Delphi — a multi-agent AI oracle system with 5 Explorer Agents independently gathering information from the internet. A result requires consensus from at least 3 of 5 agents. Disputed outcomes are escalated to human arbitrators for final resolution, ensuring accuracy and manipulation resistance.
$RAIN is available on decentralized exchanges on Arbitrum (Uniswap, Camelot) and on centralized exchanges. Connect your wallet via the button above to access the app and trade directly. You can deposit USDT, USDC, ETH, or BNB across Arbitrum, Ethereum, Base, and BNB networks.
